🔒 Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 24 June 2025  |  Last Updated: 28 February 2026

At Aio Crafters (https://www.aiocrafters.com/), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to safeguard it.

🛡️ Our Commitment: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party. Your privacy is our priority.

1. Information We Collect

We keep data collection to a minimum. Here's what we may collect:

a) Information You Provide Voluntarily:

  • If you contact us through email, we may collect your name, email address, and any other details you include in your message.
  • We only use this information to respond to your inquiry.

b) Automatically Collected Information:

  • We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous, aggregated data about website usage, such as pages visited, time spent, browser type, and device information.
  • This data helps us understand user behaviour and improve our services.
  • Google Analytics uses cookies for this purpose. You can learn more about how Google uses data at Google's Privacy & Terms.

c) Information We Do NOT Collect:

  • We do not collect passwords, payment details, or sensitive personal data.
  • Our online tools process data locally on your device — no user input from tools is stored on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide support when you contact us via email.
  • To analyze website usage patterns through Google Analytics and improve user experience.
  • To display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense (see Section 3).
  • We will not share your personal data with third parties unless required by law.
