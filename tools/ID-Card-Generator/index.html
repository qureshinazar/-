❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an ID card online?

Upload a photo, optionally add a logo, enter organization details, add fields, choose a color theme, and click Download to save as JPEG or PNG.

Can I add a company logo?

Yes! Click "Logo" to upload your company or school logo. It appears in the top-left corner of the card.

Can I choose pre-built color themes?

Yes! There are 8 themes: Corporate Blue, Forest Green, Sunset Orange, Royal Purple, Crimson Red, Teal Ocean, Slate Dark, and Golden Amber. You can also generate random gradients.

Can I customize text colors?

Yes! Use the color pickers to change organization text color, field label color, field value color, circle border color, and wave line color.

What is the difference between JPEG and PNG?

JPEG is smaller in file size, ideal for printing and sharing. PNG supports transparency and is ideal for further editing in design software.

Is this tool free and safe?

Yes, completely free. No sign-up, no watermarks. All processing runs locally in your browser — your photos and data are never uploaded.
