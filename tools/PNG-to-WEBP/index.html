Does WEBP support transparency?

Yes! WEBP fully supports transparency (alpha channel). When you convert a PNG with a transparent background to WEBP, the transparency is preserved perfectly.

At the default 80% quality setting, the quality loss is barely noticeable while the file size reduction is significant. You can increase the slider to 100% for maximum quality or lower it for even smaller files.

Yes, all modern browsers support WEBP — including Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari (14.0+), and Opera. Only very old browser versions may not support it.
