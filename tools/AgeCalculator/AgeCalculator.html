Age Calculator Tool – How to Know Your Age?

If you want to calculate your exact age or the age of any person, you can use our Age Calculator Tool. This tool calculates the time difference between your date of birth and any desired date, and shows your age in years, months, and days. It also displays your age in weeks, days, hours, minutes, and seconds.

Using this tool is very simple. Let's understand step by step how to use it.

How to Calculate Age

First, you need to select your Date of Birth. To do this, click on the input box provided in the tool and choose your birth date from the calendar. Make sure the selected date is correct so that the result remains accurate.

After that, you will see another option called "Age Calculation Date". This is the date on which you want to calculate your age. You can select today's date or any other specific date for which you want to know your age.

Once you enter both dates, click on the "Calculate Age" button. As soon as you press the button, the tool will instantly display your result. The result will appear in a separate box showing your age in years, months, and days format.

Along with this, your age will also be displayed in detailed formats such as:

  • Total months and days
  • Total weeks and days
  • Total days
  • Total hours
  • Total minutes
  • Total seconds

The interface of this tool is simple and responsive, making it easy to use on both mobile and desktop devices. This tool is especially helpful for people who want to calculate their or someone else's exact age accurately.

If you want to check again, simply select new dates and click the "Calculate Age" button once more. The tool will instantly show the updated result.

We hope this tool will be useful for you. Now you don't need to calculate your age manually — with just one click, you can get complete age details instantly!
