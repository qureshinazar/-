❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Word Counter tool work?

Simply type or paste your text into the text box. The tool instantly counts words, characters (with and without spaces), sentences, paragraphs, and estimated reading time in real-time.

Is this Word Counter free to use?

Yes, this Word & Character Counter is completely free to use. No sign-up, no downloads — just paste your text and get instant results.

Is my text stored or shared?

No. All processing happens locally in your browser. Your text is never uploaded, stored, or shared with any server.

How is reading time calculated?

Reading time is estimated based on an average reading speed of 200 words per minute (WPM), which is the commonly accepted standard for adult readers.
