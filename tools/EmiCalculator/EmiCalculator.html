How to Use the EMI Calculator

Using this EMI calculator is simple. Follow these steps:

1. Enter Loan Amount

First, enter the loan amount you plan to borrow from a bank or financial institution. For example: ₹50,000 or ₹500,000.

2. Enter Interest Rate

Next, input the annual interest rate (%) applied to your loan. For instance, if the bank offers a loan at 10% interest per year, enter 10.

3. Enter Loan Tenure

Then enter the loan tenure in months. For example, if the loan duration is 5 years, enter 60 months.

4. Click "Calculate EMI"

After entering all the details, click the Calculate EMI button. The calculator will instantly display the results.

Results You Will See

The EMI calculator provides the following information:

  • Monthly EMI – The amount you need to pay every month.
  • Total Interest Payable – The total interest you will pay over the loan period.
  • Total Payment – The complete amount you will repay including the loan principal and interest.
