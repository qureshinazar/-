💡 Indian vs International: In the Indian numbering system, commas are placed after the first 3 digits from the right, then every 2 digits (e.g., 1,23,45,678). In the International system, commas are placed every 3 digits (e.g., 12,345,678).

  4. Copy Output — Click "Copy" to copy the formatted numbers to your clipboard.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add commas to a number?

Enter your number in the input box and click "Format". The tool adds commas in the correct positions based on your selected format (Indian or International).

What is the difference between Indian and International format?

Indian format: 12,34,567 (groups of 2 after the first 3 digits). International format: 1,234,567 (groups of 3 throughout). Both are correct in their respective regions.

Can I format multiple numbers at once?

Yes! Enter each number on a separate line. The tool formats all numbers simultaneously.

Does it work with decimal numbers?

Yes, commas are only added to the integer part before the decimal point. The decimal part is left unchanged.

Can I also remove commas?

Yes! Click the "Remove Commas" button to strip commas from any formatted number — useful for pasting into calculators or code.

Is this tool free?

Yes, completely free. No sign-up needed. Everything runs locally in your browser — no data is stored or sent anywhere.
