📖 How to Use the Car Loan EMI Calculator

  1. Enter Loan Amount — Type the car loan amount you wish to borrow (in ₹). This is the car price minus your down payment.
  2. Enter Interest Rate — Provide the annual interest rate offered by your bank or dealer (e.g., 9.5%).
  3. Enter Loan Tenure — Specify the repayment period in months or years using the toggle button.
  4. Click "Calculate EMI" — Instantly see your monthly EMI, total interest, total payment, a visual breakdown, and a month-wise amortization schedule.
📐 EMI Formula:
EMI = [P x R x (1+R)^N] / [(1+R)^N - 1]

Where:
P = Principal Loan Amount (Car Price - Down Payment)
R = Monthly Interest Rate (Annual Rate / 12 / 100)
N = Total Number of Monthly Installments

💡 Tips Before Taking a Car Loan

  • Make a Larger Down Payment — A higher down payment (20–30%) reduces your loan amount, EMI, and total interest significantly.
  • Compare Rates — Don't just accept the dealer's financing. Compare rates from multiple banks and NBFCs.
  • Choose the Right Tenure — A shorter tenure (3–4 years) means higher EMI but much less total interest compared to 7 years.
  • Check Your Credit Score — A CIBIL score of 750+ helps you secure lower interest rates.
  • Factor in Total Cost — Remember to include insurance, registration, road tax, and accessories in your budget.
  • Avoid Long Tenures — Cars depreciate quickly. A 7-year loan may leave you owing more than the car's value.
  • Read the Fine Print — Check for processing fees, prepayment charges, and foreclosure penalties before signing.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How is Car Loan EMI calculated?

EMI is calculated using the reducing balance formula: EMI = [P x R x (1+R)^N] / [(1+R)^N - 1], where P is the loan amount, R is the monthly interest rate, and N is the total number of monthly installments.

What is the typical interest rate for car loans?

Car loan interest rates in India typically range from 7% to 12% for new cars and 12% to 18% for used cars, depending on the bank, credit score, and car model.

What is the ideal down payment for a car?

Most banks require a minimum down payment of 10–20% of the car's on-road price. However, making a 20–30% down payment is recommended to reduce your EMI burden and total interest cost.

Can I prepay my car loan?

Yes, most banks allow prepayment of car loans. Some may charge a prepayment penalty (typically 2–5%), while others offer zero prepayment charges. Check with your lender for details.
